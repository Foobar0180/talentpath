Interview Kits

Each company has their own way of hiring. You can create an interview kit for each assessment stage (phone screen, first, second, third interview), to ensure that every candidate is assessed consistently.

Helpful Hints

Interview kits can ONLY be built for Phone Screen, Assessment and Interview stage types.


Available Stages

Select the pipeline stage for the interview.

Phone Screen Assessment Interview

Add questions, skills or competencies to your kit.

Create your custom scorecard for this job by adding questions or importing them from templates and other jobs.

General
  • Tell me why you are interested in this job
  • What about our company interests you?
  • Do you know anyone that works for us?
  • Why did you leave your last job?
  • Why should we hire you?
Work ethic
  • Could you define for me what you believe doing a good job is? A great job?
  • In your last position, what particular task or project do you believe you did a great job with? Be specific. Explain why you feel it was great.
Timeline
  • Where are you in your job search? Who else have you applied to/got interviews with?
  • Are you actively interviewing? What other companies are you interviewing with?
  • Is there anything that would prevent you from starting a new position within a reasonable notice period?
  • What is your notice period?
Salary
  • Please walk me through your current compensation package (Base Salary, Bonus, Stock options, benefits)
  • What are your salary requirements?
Relocation/Commute
  • Would you be willing to relocate if required?
Culture Fit
  • Describe your ideal work environment
  • Walk me through your perfect work day
  • What can your hobbies tell me that your resume can't?
  • What are you passionate about?
