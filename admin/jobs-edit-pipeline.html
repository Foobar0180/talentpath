Customise your hiring pipeline

Every job requires a hiring pipeline workflow. You can set up your hiring pipeline to match your company's recruitment process. Drag and drop stages to re-arrange them. Click on stages to rename or delete them.

Helpful Hints

Custom pipeline stages can only be created by admin members of your TalentPath account.

Built-in Stages "Sourced" and "Applied"

These are default stages that TalentPath needs to manage your incoming candidates, so you cannot change or delete them. Candidates applying through your careers portal, website or job boards will be initially place in the "Applied" stage.

Candidates that you upload or pre-select from your candidate database will be placed in the "Sourced" stage. Unlike candidates who have applied themselves, sourced candidates are people you need to reach out to before establishing that they are interested in a job.

Customisable Stages

You can label stages however you like. You can assign a "stage type" for each pipeline stage. This helps with displaying the appropriate icons and also produce meaningful reports.

For example, if you have two rounds of interviews, create two stages called "Interview 1" and "Interview 2" but mark them both as type "Interview" so we can display them in the right way.
