Cross Awareness Information

If you have a LinkedIn Recruiter account, adding ‘Cross-System Awareness’ will automatically integrate candidates’ LinkedIn profile data with TalentPath.

This means you can see which LinkedIn prospects already exist in your account and candidate profile information is kept up-to-date automatically.
