Developer API

Should you need to display your jobs in different ways, or to extract additional information from your TalentPath account, our API gives you full control over your recruiting data.

Extract information about:

  • Your account
  • Your team members and external recruiters
  • Your hiring pipeline stages for each position
  • Your posted jobs
  • Screening questions

Manage your candidates:

  • Fetch all candidates or applicants for a specific job
  • Extract detailed candidate information
  • Create candidate profiles inside TalentPath
