Roles and Permissions

When a hiring team is set up for a job, the assigned team members are given a role based on their responsibility level. These roles are customisable and allow you to fine tune your hiring process.

Name Description Type Users Date Modified
Administrator
Administrators are a built-in position and by default have access to everything within TalentPath.
Built-in 1 12 Sep, 2016

Hiring Manager
Hiring Manager have full access to a job. When comments and emails are confidential they can be marked for ‘Hiring Managers’ only, which means that other team members cannot see the information discussed. This role is best assigned to the head of the hiring department and/or senior management.

Team Member
Team Members can see candidate profiles, add comments and messages and help screen candidates. They can move candidates to the next stage or disqualify those not right for the role. Team Members will not be able to see or add confidential comments or messages in the system. Make a coworker a hiring team member when you’d like them to assist in the basic evaluation and screening of candidates.

External Recruiter
External Recruiters are able to submit candidates directly into your TalentPath account, where you can review them with your hiring team, and move them through the pipeline.
