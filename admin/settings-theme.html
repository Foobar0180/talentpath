Create a branded careers site

It’s easy to create a branded careers site with TalentPath and there’s no coding required. Our intuitive online editor gives you the tools you need to create a flexible design that’s always up to date with a list of your latest positions.

Color Pallette
"Brand color" lets you set your brand's color as the dominating color on your Careers Site. Links and buttons' backgrounds will get this color.
"Text color" lets you adjust the color of the body text. Dark text is always easy to read on light background.
"Headings" lets you adjust the color of headings. Ideally headings should be darker than body text.
"Subheadings" lets you adjust the color of subheadings. Ideally subheadings should be lighter than body text.
