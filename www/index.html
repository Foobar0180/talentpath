How it works?

We give organisations the power to streamline their talent acquisition process and take in-house recruiting to a whole new level.
Find better candidates, conduct more focused interviews, and make intelligent hiring decisions.

Plan your hiring process

Plan your next hire by writing targeted job ads, create custom application forms for faster screening and schedule interviews with candidates and members of your hiring team.

Sourcing and Promotion

Source top candidates from social media sites, recruiting partners, and third party job boards with a click of a button.

Screening, Assessments and Profiling

Our advanced assessment and profiling tools ensure that your sourced candidates and talent pools are filled with the best possible candidates.

Interviewing

Schedule and invite candidates and members of your hiring team to participate in interviews. Don't worry we support both Google Mail and Microsoft Exchange.

Hiring and Onboarding

Using one of our predesigned hiring contracts or by creating your own, we make it easy to export or email hiring contracts.

Reports

Gain deeper insights into the performance of your recruiting process. Learn from past mistakes and improve any area in your future recruitment process.
