The Best Anti-Ageing Treatments For Summer 2016

post by Medikal Health Care UK on November 5th, 2016

Mr Ifran Khan, a Medikal Cosmetic Surgeon, talks about the best anti-ageing treatments for summer 2016

Unfortunately for us, ageing is just a part of life, and while there are many benefits that come with age, there are also downfalls when it comes to our appearance. As we age, our bodies slowly deteriorate in a variety of ways, which can lead to changes in our physical appearance.

  • Image 1.

  • Skin loses its elasticity, which can lead to sagging, wrinkles and creases, especially around our face and neck. It is a daily battle with issues such as crows feet, laugh lines and droopy jowls. Not only this, but years of sun exposure can lead to dry skin and age spots.

Prevention & Skin Care Treatments For Anti-Ageing

While our skin may suffer, and show for it, over time, there are ways in which you can care for your skin and prevent some of the issues we face. It is important to first establish a solid daily skincare routine in order to both care for your skin and protect it from daily aggravation and the harsh environment.

Basically, your skin goes through a lot but you can do more to look after it. Along with a good skincare routine, there are also supplements available that go towards prevention as well as helping to fix fine lines and blemishes etc.


Age with Grace

While a good skincare routine is important, skin care only goes so far, which is when you may need to consider other options to help tackle the signs of ageing further. There are a number of both surgical and non-surgical treatments and procedures that can help to rejuvenate the appearance of your skin for a more youthful and fresh look.

Some of these include:

- Dermal Fillers - Used to plump out fine lines and restore volume.
- Non-Surgical Facelifts - Used to rejuvenate skin and achieve a fresher, more youthful look but without surgery.
- Laser Resurfacing - Used for skin rejuvenation.
- Facelifts - Used to surgically enhance and lift the face.
