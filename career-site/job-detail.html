Finance & Commercial Manager

Job description

About us

Clare Park Hospital is set in an area of outstanding natural beauty, based near Farnham, Surrey. It has excellent facilities and is staffed by a team of highly skilled professionals. We offer a wide range of medical and surgical treatments supporting patients in the surrounding four counties.

 

The role

Clare Park Hospital is currently looking for an experienced and high-calibre Finance and Commercial Manager to support the Hospital Director and Senior Management Team on a full time basis, 37.5 hours per week.

 

 Responsibilities

  • Provide clear financial leadership and commercial support including month-end management accounts, developing the annual budgets and preparing the financials for the 2017-2019 Hospital Three-Year plan.
  • Join the Senior Management team, working closely with the Hospital Director, Head of Clinical Services/Matron and Business Development Manager to play a critical role in shaping short and long term business achievement
  • Ensure customer processes are effective, efficient, profit and cash flow centred
  • Support the management team in change management and on projects which influence future improved performance
  • Develop the hospital’s finance function sharing best practice with colleagues within our network of hospitals
  • To directly manage the Hospital Business Office which has responsibility for the hospital’s charging and finance processes
  • Pipeline Management

Requirements

  • Commercially astute qualified accountant
  • Healthcare sector knowledge would be good but is not essential
  • You’ll need to be PC literate and ideally conversant with SAP and Microsoft office
  • Ability to interpret financial and operating data identifying drivers to profit and drive change through P&L discussions with each Head of Department
  • Excellent interpersonal and communication skills
  • Confidence, drive, energy and enthusiasm
  • Good organisation and planning skills
  • The ability to problem solve and think laterally about a problem
  • A flexible approach to support the month-end process and other challenges as they arise in a fast-paced customer facing environment

Reward
